The Collective

Join The Collective

One Community. One Mission. Limitless Impact.

At The CCC: Coffee & Canines Collective, we believe that the best way to support our rescue mission is by surrounding ourselves with people who care about quality, sustainability, and local heritage. "The Collective" is our partnership initiative—a dedicated space for Fresno’s local bakers, farmers, artisans, and rescue organizations to join forces with us.

Whether you’re growing the ingredients we serve or providing the homes for the dogs we champion, you are the backbone of this mission.

For Local Producers & Artisans

We are constantly looking to source the highest quality local goods to feature on our menu and in our cafe. If you share our commitment to a "circular kitchen" and sustainable practices, we want to hear from you.

Growers & Ranchers: For the fresh, seasonal ingredients that define our menus.

Bakers & Producers: For the artisanal breads, pastries, and small-batch goods that keep our community fed.

Artisans & Makers: For the unique, handcrafted items that celebrate canine culture.

For Rescues & Shelters

The "Canines" in our name is a promise. We are looking to partner with local Fresno rescues and shelters to help facilitate adoptions, spread breed awareness, and provide a platform for your hardest-to-place dogs. Let’s work together to make "Adopt, Not Shop" the standard.

Let’s Build Something Together

Ready to become part of the Pack? Fill out the brief interest form below. Whether you have a product to share or a rescue mission to scale, we’ll be in touch to discuss how we can grow together.
