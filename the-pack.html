The Pack

Different Backgrounds, One Shared Heart

The CCC: Coffee & Canines Collective was born from the belief that expertise and empathy belong at the same table. Our team—The Pack—is comprised of family, friends, and professionals who have traded traditional career paths for a mission with more meaning. From high-end culinary artistry to strategic operations and analytical sustainability, we leverage our diverse skills to ensure that every cup poured and every meal served directly fuels the future of canine rescue.

James Hennessee-Bresett

President & Executive Chef

Bringing a chef’s precision to the world of animal advocacy, James combines his background in high-end culinary arts—ranging from modernist techniques to technical plating—with a lifelong devotion to canine welfare. To him, the Collective is more than a cafe; it’s a professional-grade "circular kitchen" built to sustain a rescue mission.

Maude Hernandez

Vice-President & Canine Welfare Manager

As the heart of the Collective’s welfare initiatives, Maude manages the daily operations that keep our mission moving forward. Her compassionate approach to rescue advocacy and grounded leadership ensure that every project we undertake is focused on the well-being and future of the dogs we serve.

Andreas Santucci

Treasurer & Director of Finance

A trusted friend and board member, Andreas provides the analytical rigor essential for a growing nonprofit. By lending his expertise in mathematics and economics, he ensures our model is sustainable and efficient, allowing us to dedicate the maximum amount of resources toward saving lives.

Peggy Hennessee

Secretary & Director of Canine Welfare

With a strategic eye and a deep love for all breeds, Peggy provides the operational oversight for our welfare programs. Her commitment to the cause ensures that our high-level vision translates into real-world impact for rescues, bridging the gap between planning and purposeful action.
